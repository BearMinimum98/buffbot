Buffy down? No MP left on Buffy? No problem, RU's BuffBot got you covered! Simply send a KMail and voila!

How do I use BuffBot?

Send a KMail to buffbot_user_name (#profile_id) with newlines separating the buffs (part of buff name or entire buff name). The song duration goes first, with the buff name going second. If you do not specify buff duration, default is 50 turns.

I demand an example

  • To be buffed with 100 turns of polka of plenty and 50 turns of jackasses', type in
    100 polka
    symphony
  • To be buffed with 50 turns of Ur-Kel's, type
    aria or ur-kel's

Help! I didn't get a buff I needed!

Either it was unavailable, or not enough meat was available to purchase magical mystery juice to refill MP was there.

How does Buffbot work?

BuffBot is built on top of PyKol, and is hosted by Google AppEngine.
